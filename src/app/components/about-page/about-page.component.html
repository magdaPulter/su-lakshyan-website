About
Su Lakshyan

Through vocational training for women, including computer and English language skills, Su Lakshyan broadens their employment prospects and thereby empowers them, including financially and this way breaks the cycle of poverty.

By generating local employment opportunities, it influences economic growth and stability of the community. Joint projects integrate the community.

Recycling and production of eco-friendly bags positively impacts environmental issues, while acting in the spirit of Nepali culture by producing Dhaka-related goods.

So Su Lakshyan is creating a future where women not only benefit directly, but actively contribute to building an inclusive, empowered and sustainable society, inspiring positive change beyond enterprise boundaries.

Organization’s goal

We focus on giving women wings, which is why we have a mission to empower 1500 underprivileged women in Pokhara, Nepal, by 2025, achieving sustainable impact through measurable goals.

ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT

80% of program graduates secure sustainable employment or start their own businesses within one year

ENVIROMENTAL SUSTAINABILITY

80% of program graduates secure sustainable employment or start their own businesses within one year

CULTURAL PRESERVATION

Sustain the production of Nepali Dhaka-related goods, with a 50% increase in market demand

DIGITAL INCLUSION

90% of participants demonstrate improved proficiency in basic computer skills and English language

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

Form partnerships with at least three local businesses and NGOs.

GENDER EQUALITY AND EMPOWERMENT

70% of participants report increased agency and support for their aspirations.
